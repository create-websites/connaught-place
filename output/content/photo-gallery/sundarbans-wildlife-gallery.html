About this page

This website showcases a collection of images from the Sundarbans in India, focusing on wildlife travel. The layout is clean and responsive, with descriptive alt text on each image for increased accessibility and search visibility.

Each gallery photo includes responsive srcset image widths 100, 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 900, and 1000 are specified in the markup. In addition, the page features SEO metadata, Open Graph and Twitter tags, and structured JSON-LD data for search and generative discovery systems.
